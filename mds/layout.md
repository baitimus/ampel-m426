# Ampel Board Layout

This document describes the GPIO pin layout for the traffic light (Ampel) connections on the Raspberry Pi Pico W board.

## Ampel 1: Autos (Cars)
Controls the traffic light for vehicles.

* **Red Light:** GPIO 6
* **Yellow Light:** GPIO 7
* **Green Light:** GPIO 8
* **Ground (GND):** Any available GND pin

## Ampel 2: Fußgänger (Pedestrians)
Controls the traffic light for pedestrians.

* **Red Light:** GPIO 18
* **Yellow Light:** GPIO 19
* **Green Light:** GPIO 20
* **Ground (GND):** Any available GND pin
